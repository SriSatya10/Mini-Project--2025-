About Therabot

Our Mission

At Therabot, we believe that mental health support should be accessible, empathetic, and available 24/7. Our mission is to democratize mental wellness by providing a safe, judgment-free space where you can express your thoughts and feelings anytime, anywhere.

Why We Built Therabot

In today's fast-paced world, many people struggle with stress, anxiety, and depression but lack immediate access to support. Traditional therapy can be expensive, and appointments are often weeks away. We built Therabot to bridge that gap—offering instant, evidence-based coping strategies and compassionate listening when you need it most.
