Platform Features

💬

Interactive Chat

Engage in a guided, conversational experience that helps you navigate your feelings. The structured interface carefully asks questions to understand your situation and provides supportive, empathetic feedback.

📊

Clinical Assessments

Take scientifically-backed assessments (like PTSD, OCD, Anxiety, Depression) to better understand your mental health and track your progress over time.

☁️

Seamless Cloud Sync

Your chat history and assessment scores are automatically synced to your secure database profile, so you never lose your progress and can review past sessions.

🔒

Secure & Private

Your data is yours. We use secure user authentication and isolated database records to ensure that your conversations remain strictly confidential.
