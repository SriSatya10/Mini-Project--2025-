How Therabot Helps

✅ What Therabot Does

  • ✔ Emotional Support: Provides empathetic responses and active listening.
  • ✔ Wellness Guidance: Offers tips and resources for mental well-being.
  • ✔ Personalized Insights: Learns from your interactions to provide tailored support.
  • ✔ Privacy First: Ensures your conversations are kept confidential.
  • ✔ Always Available: Offers 24/7 support whenever you need it.
  • ✔ Mood Tracking: Helps you monitor and understand your emotional patterns.

❌ What Therabot Doesn’t Do

  • ✖ Replace Therapy: Not a substitute for professional mental health care.
  • ✖ Diagnose Conditions: Does not provide medical diagnoses.
  • ✖ Prescribe Medication: Cannot prescribe or recommend medications.
  • ✖ Handle Emergencies: Not equipped to handle crisis situations.
  • ✖ Share Data: Will not share your data with third parties.

    About Therabot

    Therabot is designed to provide emotional support and wellness guidance. It's a tool to help you reflect on your feelings and find resources for mental well-being. We prioritize your privacy and ensure your conversations are confidential.

    Our goal is to make mental health support accessible to everyone, anytime. While Therabot can be a helpful companion, it’s important to remember it’s not a replacement for professional therapy.

    Therabot Features

    • Emotional Support: Empathetic responses and active listening.
    • Wellness Guidance: Tips and resources for mental well-being.
    • Personalized Insights: Tailored support based on your interactions.
    • Mood Tracking: Monitor your emotional patterns.
    • 24/7 Availability: Support whenever you need it.
